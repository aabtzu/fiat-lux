```
ASNS 1150
East Asian Studies
Snell Engineering Center 108
Mon, Wed, Thu: 1:35 pm - 2:40 pm

ARCH 1310
Buildings and Cities, A Global History
Richards Hall 300
Tue, Fri: 9:50 am - 11:30 am

ARCH 1311
Recitation for ARCH 1310
Ryder Hall 207
Tue: 11:45 am - 12:50 pm

ARCH 2240
Architectonic Systems
Ryder Hall 215
Mon, Thu: 11:45 am - 1:25 pm

ARCH 1000
Architecture at Northeastern
West Village G 106
Mon: 10:30 am - 11:35 am

ARCH 2130
Site, Space, Program
Architecture Studio 100
Tue, Fri: 1:35 pm - 5:05 pm
```

Fiat Lux parses the course codes, titles, locations, and time slots — and generates an interactive weekly calendar:
